How to Use This Tool (30-second guide)

  1. Quick add: Tap a symptom button above (Motor Decline, Seizures, etc.) to pre-fill the form, then adjust severity and add notes.
  2. Full entry: Open “Add Symptom Entry”, pick a date, category, symptom name, severity (1–10 slider), and any notes about triggers or what helped.
  3. Watch patterns: The dashboard at the top shows your most frequent symptom, average severity trend, and days tracked.
  4. Share with your doctor: Tap “Print for Doctor” to get a clean printout, or “Copy Summary” to paste into a message.
  5. Back up regularly: Your data lives in this browser only. Tap “Download Backup” after every few entries. Keep the file safe.
Tip: Tracking symptoms consistently — even brief entries — helps your care team spot patterns and adjust treatment. Noting what helped is just as valuable as noting what happened.
