Symptom Navigator

What is this tool?
This guided assessment helps you identify what your child is experiencing, flags symptoms that need urgent attention, and directs you to the specific pages on this site that address your concerns. Check the symptoms you are observing, then click Get My Results for personalized recommendations. All processing happens in your browser — nothing is stored or sent anywhere.

How to Use This Tool (30-second guide)

  1. Fill in your child's profile: Age, MLD type, diagnosis status, and treatment status. This helps personalize the results.
  2. Check symptoms: Open each category (Motor, Neurological, Feeding, etc.) and check everything your child experiences — current or historical. A severity selector appears for each.
  3. Click "Get My Results": The tool generates red-flag alerts (what needs attention now), recommended reading pages, specialist gap analysis, and suggested tests.
  4. Save or print results: Use the Copy, Download, or Print buttons at the bottom of your results to share with your doctor.
  5. Come back anytime: Nothing is saved — you can re-run this whenever symptoms change. It takes about 5 minutes.
Tip: This tool does not store any data. Form state is lost on page refresh by design — your privacy is protected.
